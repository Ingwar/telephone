We are researchers at University of Wisconsin-Madison who are interested in studying the process of how audio messages get passed on from person to person, much like in the childrenâ€™s game Telephone. If you choose to participate in our study, we will ask you to listen to an audio message recorded by someone else, and then record yourself imitating the message that you heard.

Our game differs from the children's game in that many of the sounds you will hear will not be recognizable as English words. Your task is the same: to recreate the sound you heard as accurately as you can.

The interface below is used to play the game. Clicking on the top sound wave icon will play a sound, and clicking on the bottom sound wave will toggle your computer's microphone recorder so that you can record your own.

After you have submit your entry, the next person who arrives at the site will now hear your recording, and record their own recreation of your recreation (and so on, and so on!). Don't worry, we will not ask you to provide any individuating information in your recordings. Each player will be asked to recreate a few messages before completion.
